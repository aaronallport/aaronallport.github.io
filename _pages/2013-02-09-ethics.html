Ethics

Everyone has rules that they work to, and I am no exception. There are certain projects types that I do not work on simply because I do not agree with what they represent. These include:

  • Anything to do with pro-weapons or armament
  • Anything that uses public money for private gain

It isn't all doom and gloom...

Now that we've got the "won't do's" out of my system, I would like to draw attention to something I do care about, and in-fitting with the ethics that I work to. I set aside a couple of pro-bono days each month for charity and other non-profit work. If you are a charity or non-profit organisation and would like to work with me, then please send me an email.
